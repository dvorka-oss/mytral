TRIMP: TRaining IMPulse

Training Impulse (TRIMP) quantifies internal training load from session duration and heart-rate reserve.

Dots show daily TRIMP - < 50 recovery/easy 50 - 120 moderate > 120 hard - while ATRIMP (Acute TRIMP, 7-day exponential moving average), CTRIMP (Chronic TRIMP, 42-day exponential moving average) and TRIMPB (TRIMP balance CTRIMP - ATRIMP) shows balance between short-term load, long-term load i.e. delta between fitness and fatigue.
