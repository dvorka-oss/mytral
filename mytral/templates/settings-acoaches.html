LLMs are the specific AI engines provided by your chosen LLM providers to power your coaches. You can assign a different model to each coach based on your needs. For local LLM providers, it is recommended to use high-performance open-source models like GPT-OSS or Qwen, which are optimized for machines with a normal amount of VRAM. For third-party providers, please note that apart from potential loss of privacy, larger models may increase both costs and response latency.
