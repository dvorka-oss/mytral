How to Read This Chart

Each dot represents one activity positioned by its strain scores in three independent energy-system dimensions:

  • SS CP (X axis) — oxidative / aerobic strain. Endurance and tempo workouts score high here.
  • SS W′ (Y axis) — glycolytic strain. Interval and threshold workouts score high here.
  • SS Pmax (Z axis) — PCr / explosive strain. Sprint and neuromuscular workouts score high here.

Natural clusters emerge: endurance rides group near the CP axis, intervals spread across CP + W′, and sprint-heavy sessions extend along the Pmax axis. Activities of similar type tend to cluster together, making it easy to spot outliers or shifts in training composition.

Dot color reflects total strain score (SS total): easy (SS < 50), moderate (SS 50–150), hard (SS > 150).

Interact — drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, and hover over any dot to see activity details.
