Activities Datasets ({{ form.data_files.data|length }})

Activities are the main items managed by Mytral. They are stored in datasets. Each dataset is a collection of activities. You can create new datasets, add activities to them, and delete activities from them. You can also delete datasets. Deleting a dataset will delete all activities in it. All datasets are merged to a single in-memory dataset called lifelong which provides comprehensive view on your life in sport. lifelong is a symbolic dataset name which indicates that you work with all activities in all year datasets.
