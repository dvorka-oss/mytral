ML Models (TabPFN)

TabPFN is an in-context learning model that turns your personal training history into instant predictions — no training step needed. It analyses your activity data directly and uses it as context for predictions like illness risk, fatigue, and performance trends.

Model: TabPFN v2 (Apache 2.0, ~100 MB). Weights are downloaded once from HuggingFace and cached locally. Your training data never leaves your device.

Requirement: install the optional ml dependency group: uv sync --group ml
