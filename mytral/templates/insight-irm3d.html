3D Impulse-Response Model

3D Impulse-Response Model is a novel approach to modeling training load and performance in endurance sports. It models training load in 3 independent dimensions using strain score (SS): Explosive Power (Pmax) Aero Generator (CP), Prime Battery (W′), and .

Workouts with power recordings are converted second-by-second into SSCP / SSW′ / SSPmax, then aggregated daily and fed into parallel impulse-response models.
