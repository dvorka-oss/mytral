3D Impuls-Response Model Activity Analysis

Activity Details

3D Impulse-Response Model is a novel approach to modeling training load and performance in endurance sports. It models training load in 3 independent dimensions using the Strain Score (SS):

  • Explosive Power (Pmax) — strain on the phosphocreatine system
  • Aero Generator (CP) — strain on the oxidative system
  • Prime Battery (W′) — strain on the glycolytic system

Workouts with power recordings are converted second-by-second into SS CP / SS W′ / SS Pmax, then aggregated daily and fed into parallel impulse-response models.
