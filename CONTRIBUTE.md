# Contribute
_"The important is - is your code good? I care about excellent code. I don't care who you are. Maybe you are a dog. I don't care! I don't care where are you come from. I do care about the quality of your code, because this is what defines our community."_ -- Jean-Baptiste Kempf (VLC)

The current MyTraL implementation is just an initial, imperfect sketch of a much broader vision. Its purpose is to demonstrate the viability of a personalizable training log where the community crowdsources content and customizations - including import/export capabilities for various formats, metrics, exercises, visualizations, and more.

Frankly, MyTraL is currently a mixture of handwritten core functionality blended with AI-assisted code, which I refactor and rewrite whenever I have the capacity. At this stage of the project, the priority is features and vision rather than performance and perfect code, though I hope to get there eventually. That said, Jean-Baptiste Kempf’s philosophy still applies: whether you use AI tools or write everything by hand, what matters most is that I can understand and integrate your contributions.

Feel free to contribute! 

* **Bugs and Suggestions**
  * ... submit bugs, issues, ideas, and enhancements.
* **Content**
  * ... add exercises, metrics, and other features to be available in MyTraL as extensions.
* **Translations**
  * ... translate MyTraL into your language.
* **Platform Support**
  * ... port MyTraL to your favorite OS or distribution.
* **Code**
  * ... submit a pull request or a patch with the implementation of a feature you miss.
* **Research**
  * ... consider conducting your AI/ML, NLP, or data mining research on top of (your) training data normalized by MyTraL.
* **Integration**
  * ... share a how-to guide or code enabling integration with your favorite project.
* **Enhancements**
  * ... submit performance, efficiency, and/or productivity enhancements.
* **Documentation**
  * ... create a YouTube video, write a blog post, tweet, or help build our guides.

Don't hesitate to contact me - I will be happy to talk!
